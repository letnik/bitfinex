Advanced order types

We know that entering multiple orders repeatedly is not efficient and can be very tedious. To streamline this task, we created the Scaled Orders tool for our users. It’s an algorithmic order type designed to enable traders to focus more on strategy instead of spending the time manually entering orders. The tool enables our customers to automatically create multiple limit orders across a user-determined price range.

At the same time, traders still have control as they can set the diversity and the distribution of the orders. Check out our Support page for further details and a tutorial on Scaled Orders.

Paper Trading

Bitfinex’s Paper Trading feature allows traders to test trading strategies in a simulated market environment without the needs to deposit real funds. It works on sub-accounts, which emulate the master account’s trading functionalities.

Learn how to create Paper Trading accounts

Honey Framework

Creating custom order types and event-driven automated trading strategies is no longer the preserve of specialist algorithmic trading firms. We have made these strategies accessible to our customers through the Honey Framework integration. What’s more, our latest development has made it easier to use, even without any coding knowledge or developer know-how.
